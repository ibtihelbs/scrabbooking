Our Story

We believe great seafood starts with respect — for the ocean, the catch, and the people around the table.

Working closely with local fishermen and suppliers, we serve seafood at its freshest, prepared simply and thoughtfully.

Whether it’s a quick lunch or a long evening with friends, our goal is the same: honest food and a warm welcome.
